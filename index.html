Westborough Hacks

A highschool hackathon on Feb 15-16 in Westborough, MA!

What is this?

This is a hackathon: a 24-hour event where students of all backgrounds, interests, and talents will come together and be able to create whatever their mind can think of. We will provide whatever you may need, from mentorships and workshops to free food and a place to sleep.

Why Westborough?

Westborough has been at the forefront of education for decades, however, it is severely lacking in it's adaptation of computer science. As students of Westborough High School, we feel as though there is a severe under-utilization of resources and are aiming to fix that.

Why should I come?

Why not?! It's just one weekend full of free-food, hanging out with friends, and just having fun. Best-case scenario, you find out that you have a passion for computer science. Worst-case scenario, you just spend a weekend hanging out with friends and have a ton of free food and snacks.
