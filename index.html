Westborough Hacks

A highschool hackathon on Feb 29-20 in Westborough, MA!

What is this?

This is a hackathon: a 24-hour event where students of all backgrounds, interests, and talents will come together and be able to create whatever their mind can think of. We will provide whatever you may need, from mentorships and workshops to free food and a place to sleep.

Why Westborough?

Westborough has been at the forefront of education for decades, however, it is severely lacking in it's adaptation of computer science. As students of Westborough High School, we feel as though there is a severe under-utilization of resources and are aiming to fix that.

Why should I come?

Why not?! It's just one weekend full of free-food, hanging out with friends, and just having fun. Best-case scenario, you find out that you have a passion for computer science. Worst-case scenario, you just spend a weekend hanging out with friends and have a ton of free food and snacks.

FAQs

Who can come?

Any highschooler that wants to come! We are expecting people from the metro-west area to come, but if you can get yourself to the event, you are welcome to join!

What do I bring?

Make sure that you bring your student ID so that we can check you in. You probably want to bring your computer, chargers, and anything else you might need to hack. If you require them, think about bringing some toiletries.

How much does it cost?

FREE!! 100% free. You don't have to pay a dime to get in. We will provide everything from swag to food at no cost to you.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem! We will have workshops to get you started and mentors will be around to help you with any questions that you may have.

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Yup! We will be using the MLH Code of Conduct and all attendees will be required to abide by it.

Do you provide travel reimbursements?

We will not be providing travel reimbursements. However, if you feel as though you would really like to come, but are not able to, check out Execute Big's Travel Grant.
